FAQ

Can I put my artwork on display?

Yes, you can. You can fill out the Contact Form in the Contact Us page and we will reach back to you via email.


How do you determine your pricing?

To determine the pricing of our art pieces, we work very closely with the respective artists. Each artist typically has an hourly rate they charge for the time spent working on the art along with the cost of supplies. Prior to making the final pricing decision, we also have hire our own appraiser that adjusts pricing as need be.

What changes were made due to the COVID-19 pandemic?

We have implemented safety precautions due to the pandemic. Our opening hours have been shortened. Our Collection page has a variety of artworks that are from other cultures.
