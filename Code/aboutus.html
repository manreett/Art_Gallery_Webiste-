We are an art gallery located Oshawa,Ontario just an hour east of Toronto. We offer art from paintings to sculptures to hand made merchandise made from local artist. We often sell this artwork where all proceeds go the artist looking to grow their business and in exchange you could own a art piece from a soon to be famous artist! Price is also negotiable! The art gallery is free and open to all ages so everyone can enjoy and learn about art. The inspiration during Covid 19 we have decide to take our business online too!
